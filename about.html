About NewsFrame

Empowering journalists and social-media editors to produce compelling, professional news graphics — fast.

Built for News Professionals

NewsFrame is a browser-based thumbnail editor designed specifically for social-media news teams. No software to install, no subscription required — open the page, upload your photo, add your headline, and download.

Whether you publish in English, Urdu, Arabic, or any other language, NewsFrame's Unicode-aware text engine renders your copy beautifully with proper RTL support.

  • Drag-and-drop image upload with instant canvas preview
  • Automatic channel logo overlay, repositionable and resizable
  • Multi-language text tool with Urdu & Arabic RTL support
  • Rich text formatting: font, size, color, bold, italic, underline, alignment
  • One-click export as PNG or JPG — pixel-perfect output
  • Unlimited undo / redo — never worry about mistakes
  • Fully responsive — works on mobile and desktop

NewsFrame runs entirely in your browser using the powerful Fabric.js canvas library. Your images never leave your device — everything is processed locally, keeping your content private and secure.

Designed with news media teams in mind, the dark-blue interface minimises eye strain during long editing sessions while keeping the focus on your content.
